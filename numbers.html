Financial Model

Baseline, growth, and the split — in plain terms.

Overall current baseline

Overall current bar baseline in the model is $693,000 (normalized Wed–Sun weekly average, full season including slow April/October) — walk-in and regular bar nights only. Private events and buyouts are not included in that number; the deck uses a separate $5,000 event-revenue line. Baseline stays 100% yours on the bar. The day-by-day uplift table sums to about $1.37M bar revenue at target; the slider uses a straight 2× on bar baseline (~$1.39M) for the multiplier math.

Incremental revenue

Everything above baseline — from bar growth, events, and overall uplift — is tracked as new revenue in the model. Merch is not included in those figures. The interactive slider on the main proposal shows total season bar revenue at your chosen multiplier. Any split on growth or events is agreed in person — not fixed in this deck.

Compensation (bar)

Definitions (gross vs. net, exclusions, timing) belong in whatever you sign — this page is the conceptual map.

Compensation (events)

Event revenue is modeled separately from the bar baseline. Fees or splits on events are TBD when you meet.

Multi-year view

Year 1 proves the system; Years 2–3 compound audience, repeat visits, and event calendar. The model can be extended with season-over-season assumptions — we’ll align those with your actual books and POS reporting.
