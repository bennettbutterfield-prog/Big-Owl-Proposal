Deep-dive

Every Night Has a Number

Programming, traffic, and marketing aligned so each open night — and two new ones — has a clear target.

Opening Monday & Tuesday

Today the dock only sits empty Monday and Tuesday — you’re closed, so $0 revenue those nights. The plan is to open them and build to $2,000/day each with themed programming, local promos, early-week drink features, and content that fills the pier on the two nights it’s quiet today.

Wednesday & Thursday (already open)

Wednesday and Thursday move from “open” to optimized: higher covers, better check averages, and repeat visits driven by calendar-worthy moments (not just discounts).

Weekend growth

Friday through Sunday don’t need more random volume — they need capacity discipline and premium experiences so each hour earns more. The targets in the main table ($12.5K→$17.5K Fri & Sat, $6.5K→$11.5K Sun) assume you’re still running a tight ship, not over-crowding the experience.

The staircase to $1.37M

Baseline (Wed–Sun open) → open Mon & Tue at $2K/night each → grow Wed & Thu → weekend growth stacks to a $1,365,000 season bar revenue line at $56.8K/week. That’s the uplift story in one ladder; marketing and events then add upside on top.
