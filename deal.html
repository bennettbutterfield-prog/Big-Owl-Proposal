Term Sheet & Structure

What each side commits to — and what “success” means.

Big Owl (owner / operator)

  • Run the bar with the same quality and safety standards you use today.
  • Approve programming and major events before they go public.
  • Share weekly revenue totals so growth is measurable — reporting protected under NDA.
  • Agree on compensation for growth and events — structure and timing discussed in person.

Marketing / execution side

  • Fund all agreed ad spend and paid media.
  • Produce content: photo, video, edit, post, community management.
  • Drive influencer and event outreach; coordinate event marketing.
  • Own Google/Maps optimization and merch redesign with your approval.

Protections

Baseline revenue is protected. Fees apply to incremental revenue only. The deck references a 90-day evaluation window — exact legal language and exit mechanics get finalized in the contract.
