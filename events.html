Deep-dive

Events & Private Buyouts

Weddings, corporate groups, and buyouts — the Eastern Shore’s most under-monetized channel for a waterfront tiki bar.

Why this fits Big Owl

Boat access, sunset, and a clear “party” identity make you a destination, not a sidebar. Planners and couples are already searching for “after-party” and “rehearsal” venues within 30 miles — the goal is to show up first and close fast.

Package types

  • Wedding after-parties — higher buyouts, late hours, DJ-friendly layout.
  • Rehearsal dinners — smaller groups, strong margins on bar packages.
  • Bachelor / bachelorette — high spend per head, organic social reach.
  • Corporate & team — weekday and shoulder-season opportunities.

Revenue model (proposal)

The $693K bar baseline in the main deck is POS / walk-in bar only — events are not baked into those day-by-day numbers. For underwriting, the proposal uses a separate $5,000 season event revenue plug. Any fee or split on events is agreed in person. Real bookings can outpace the plug once marketing and packages are live.

Operations

Inquiry → contract → deposit → run-of-show → night-of execution. Marketing drives the funnel; the venue delivers the experience. All event revenue flows to Big Owl first, then the agreed split.
