The Untapped Goldmine

The Eastern Shore Does
Thousands of Weddings a Year.
None of Their After-Parties Are Here. Yet.

Here's something your competition hasn't figured out: the most lucrative guests aren't the ones who walk in off the street. They're the ones who reserve the entire bar. The Eastern Shore — St. Michaels, Oxford, Kent Island, Tilghman Island — hosts some of the most sought-after waterfront weddings on the East Coast. Every single couple who gets married within 30 miles of this dock needs an after-party venue. Right now, they're going somewhere else. A tiki bar on the water, with a dock they can arrive by boat? That's not a bar. That's a destination.
